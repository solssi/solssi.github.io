Introduction

Screw it Apps develops scorekeeper and companion apps for Android and iOS. We respect your privacy and aim to collect only what is needed to provide, improve, and monetize our apps responsibly.

By using our apps or visiting this site, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services. For questions, contact us at screwitapps@gmail.com.

Information we collect

The type of information depends on how you use our apps and your device settings. We may collect the following categories:

Data stored on your device

Game scores, player names, preferences, and similar content you enter are stored locally on your device. We do not require an account to use our scorekeeper apps. Uninstalling an app typically removes this local data unless you have backed it up through your device or platform tools.

Device and usage information

We and our service providers may collect information such as device type, operating system version, app version, language, general region (e.g., country), and interactions with app features (e.g., screens viewed, session duration). This helps us understand performance, fix issues, and improve the user experience.

Crash and diagnostic data

If you use apps that include analytics or crash reporting (for example, Firebase Crashlytics), we may receive crash logs, stack traces, and related diagnostic data. This information is used to identify and fix bugs and stability problems.

Advertising-related data

Apps that show ads may use Google AdMob or similar partners. They may collect or receive advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID or Apple’s Identifier for Advertisers), IP address, device information, and data about ad interactions to deliver and measure ads, prevent fraud, and comply with consent requirements. Where required by law, we request your consent before personalized advertising.

How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide core app functionality (keeping scores and settings on your device)
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our apps and website
  • Diagnose crashes, errors, and performance issues
  • Display advertising and, where permitted, personalize ads
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms
  • Respond to support requests you send us

We do not sell your personal information. We process data based on legitimate interests (app operation and improvement), performance of a contract (providing the app), consent (where required for ads or certain analytics), and legal obligations.

Third-party services

Our apps may integrate services operated by third parties. Their use of data is governed by their own policies. Common integrations include:

  • Google Play Services — distribution, updates, and platform features on Android (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob — advertising on supported apps (AdMob & user data)
  • Firebase (Google) — analytics, crash reporting, and related tools where enabled (Firebase Privacy)
  • Apple — App Store distribution, in-app purchases where offered, and platform services on iOS (Apple Privacy Policy)

These providers may process data in the United States and other countries. We choose partners that help us meet platform and legal requirements for mobile apps.

Advertising and cookies

Some of our apps display ads. Ad partners may use cookies, mobile advertising identifiers, and similar technologies to show ads, limit how often you see an ad, and measure campaign effectiveness.

European Economic Area (EEA), UK, and Switzerland: Where required, we use a consent mechanism (such as Google’s User Messaging Platform / GDPR consent flow for AdMob) so you can choose whether to allow personalized advertising and related storage. You can change your choices later in the app or through your device settings.

Your device settings: On Android, you can reset or limit ad personalization in Google Settings → Ads. On iOS, you can limit ad tracking in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. These controls affect how ad partners use identifiers; they may not remove all ads.

This website is primarily informational. If we use analytics on the site in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Crash logs and analytics are typically retained for a limited period needed for debugging and trend analysis, after which they are deleted or aggregated. Advertising partners retain data according to their policies and legal requirements.
