Support

Need help with one of our apps, found a bug, or want to send feedback? Contact us by email — we are happy to help.

screwitapps@gmail.com

When you write to us, please include:

  • The app name (for example, Yahtzee Score Tracker, Truco, or Carioca)
  • Your device model and OS version (iOS or Android)
  • The app version (shown in Settings or About inside the app)
  • A short description of the issue and steps to reproduce it, if applicable

We usually reply within 2–3 business days.

Frequently asked questions

Are your apps free? Our scorekeeper apps are free to download and use. Some features may be supported by optional ads.
How do I save a game? During play, use the save option in the app to keep your current scores. You can return later and continue where you left off.
How do I view past games? Open History from the home screen to review completed matches and scores.
Where is my data stored? Game data is stored locally on your device. We do not require an account to use the scorekeeper apps.
How do I report a bug? Email screwitapps@gmail.com with the app name, device details, app version, and what happened. Screenshots are helpful.
How do I request a feature? Send your suggestion to screwitapps@gmail.com. We read every message.
